# Stock Framework

Use this when the asset is a stock, equity, ADR, listed company, or publicly traded operating business.

## Core Rule

Do not force crypto-native metrics onto equities.

For stocks, the report should answer two linked but separate questions:

1. 公司业务会不会变强
2. 股票是否已经提前计价，或能否通过每股价值真正受益

A company can grow revenue while the stock underperforms because valuation is too rich, margins disappoint, dilution is high, or free cash flow conversion is weak.

## What To Emphasize

- business model and segment mix
- revenue drivers and end-market demand
- gross margin, operating margin, and operating leverage
- EPS, free cash flow, cash conversion, and capital intensity
- balance sheet, net cash or debt, and financing risk
- capital allocation: buybacks, SBC, convertibles, ATM, M&A, dividends
- valuation versus close peers
- management credibility, guidance quality, and execution history
- product cycle, capex cycle, and customer concentration
- policy, export controls, tariffs, and accounting or listing risks

## Stock Info And Metrics Near The Top

In `股票信息与核心指标`, try to include the most decision-useful metrics:

- ticker and exchange
- core business description
- market cap and enterprise value
- TTM and forward revenue
- revenue growth
- gross margin and operating margin
- EPS and free cash flow
- net cash or net debt
- diluted share count
- buyback, SBC, or convertible context
- valuation multiples such as `P/E`, `EV/Sales`, `EV/EBITDA`, `P/FCF`

If some data is unavailable, say so explicitly.

## How To Map The Existing Report Sections

- `解锁与供给压力快照`
  For stocks, reinterpret this as share dilution and capital-structure pressure: SBC, warrants, convertibles, ATM programs, secondary offerings, and buybacks.
- `3. 经济模型`
  For stocks, explain business model, revenue mix, cost structure, margins, capital intensity, and capital structure.
- `5. 捕获价值分析`
  For stocks, explain how revenue turns into gross profit, operating income, EPS, free cash flow, and shareholder return.
- `13. 股市的影响`
  For stocks, explain factor exposure, index flow sensitivity, style rotation, and peer multiple transmission.
- `15. 区块浏览器地址`
  For stocks, replace this with investor-relations and filing links.
- `16. 智能合约开发用什么语言`
  Usually `不适用` for stocks unless the company is itself a software or protocol business where the stack is materially important.

## Good Questions

- What actually drives revenue over the next 12 months?
- What margin assumptions are embedded in the current valuation?
- Is growth converting into EPS and free cash flow, or getting lost to capex and dilution?
- Are buybacks offsetting SBC, or is the share count still expanding?
- Which peers deserve a higher or lower multiple, and why?

## Common Mistakes

- using tokenomics language without translating it into share count, dilution, buybacks, and balance-sheet reality
- citing revenue growth without checking gross margin, operating margin, and cash conversion
- using non-GAAP figures without stating that they are non-GAAP
- ignoring stock-based compensation and diluted share count
- comparing with famous stocks instead of the closest operating peers
