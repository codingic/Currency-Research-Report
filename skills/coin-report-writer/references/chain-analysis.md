# Chain Analysis

Use this when the user asks about a chain, network, or L1/L2 future development prospects.

## Core Rule

Do not collapse `链的发展前景` and `代币的投资前景` into the same statement.

A chain can grow in users, developers, apps, and stablecoin liquidity while the token captures limited value. A strong report must separate:

1. 链是否在变强
2. 原生代币是否真正受益

## What To Emphasize For Chain Outlook

When the request is chain-centric, give extra weight to:

- developer growth and app diversity
- user growth and usage quality
- stablecoin and liquidity depth
- fee generation and fee sustainability
- security and decentralization
- node count, validator count, client diversity, and concentration by stake, cloud, or geography
- roadmap credibility
- interoperability and infrastructure support
- ecosystem stickiness and switching costs
- regulatory exposure at the network level
- competition with the closest chains or rollups

## What To Emphasize For Token Capture

Assess separately:

- whether fees accrue to token holders, validators, stakers, or treasury
- whether inflation, unlocks, or incentive spending offset ecosystem growth
- whether the token is required for real economic activity or mostly for speculation/governance
- whether stablecoins, sequencer economics, or app-level tokens absorb value that the native token does not

## Good Chain-Centric Questions

- Why would developers choose this chain over alternatives?
- Why would users stay once incentives fade?
- What is the moat: liquidity, culture, tooling, performance, regulation, distribution, or brand?
- What part of ecosystem growth actually reaches the native token?
- What could make the chain relevant while the token underperforms?

## Common Mistakes

- equating TVL growth with durable chain success
- equating chain activity with native-token value capture
- ignoring stablecoin-led activity that produces weak token demand
- using only throughput metrics without asking whether the activity is economically meaningful
- calling a chain decentralized or resilient without node/validator evidence
