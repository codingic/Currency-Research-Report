# Angle Framework

Use this framework to make `全方位多角度分析` concrete instead of generic.

## Core Angles

Cover as many of these as materially relevant. Aim to cover at least 8 angles in a normal report.

1. 技术架构
   consensus, throughput, latency, modularity, interoperability, execution environment, data availability, security assumptions, node topology, client diversity
2. 产品与需求
   what problem the project solves, who needs it, whether demand is real or mostly narrative
3. 用户与采用
   user growth, retention, transaction behavior, app usage, developer adoption, enterprise or institutional traction
4. 经济模型与供需
   issuance, unlocks, staking, burn, treasury, fee distribution, holder structure, sell pressure
5. 价值捕获
   whether token holders actually benefit from network usage, cash-flow analogs, fee capture, governance premium
6. 竞争格局
   closest substitutes, switching costs, moat, whether the project is differentiated or becoming commoditized
7. 治理与团队
   founder influence, governance quality, treasury discipline, roadmap credibility, execution history
8. 安全与风险
   smart contract risk, bridge risk, node or validator concentration, client monoculture, governance attack surface, dependency risk
9. 流动性与市场结构
   exchange depth, derivatives, unlock overhang, whale concentration, ETF or fund flow sensitivity, basis/funding signals when relevant
10. 社区与叙事
    organic builder/user culture vs mercenary capital, X sentiment, meme strength, community cohesion
11. 宏观敏感度
    beta to BTC, beta to rates/liquidity, dollar strength sensitivity, reaction to risk-on/risk-off regimes
12. 监管与政策
    securities risk, exchange listing risk, geographic concentration, policy tailwinds/headwinds
13. AI 相关性
    real AI utility, compute/data relevance, infra fit, or pure narrative spillover
14. 地域与生态依赖
    dependence on specific jurisdictions, ecosystems, wallets, exchanges, clouds, or partners
15. 地缘政治与战争敏感度
    sanctions exposure, energy-price sensitivity, safe-haven behavior, cyber conflict risk, regional liquidity disruption, payment-fragmentation demand

## Writing Pattern

For each selected angle, answer these two questions:

1. What is the current state?
2. Why does it matter for valuation, adoption, token demand, or downside risk?

## Output Style

- Prefer short labeled bullets or a compact matrix in section 8.
- Mark clearly when an angle is `强`, `中性`, `弱`, or `不适用`.
- Do not force all 15 angles if some are genuinely irrelevant; mark `不适用` and explain briefly.
