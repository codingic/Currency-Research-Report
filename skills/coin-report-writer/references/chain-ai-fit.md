# Chain AI Fit

Use this when the user asks how a chain should best combine with AI or what the best AI use case is for a chain.

## Core Rule

Do not answer with generic slogans like `AI + blockchain has huge potential`.

The task is to identify:

1. which AI direction best fits this chain
2. why it fits this chain better than other AI directions
3. how it could actually land as a product or ecosystem strategy
4. whether the chain's native token would benefit

## Candidate AI Directions

Test the chain against these possible directions:

1. AI agent payments and wallets
   good fit when the chain is cheap, fast, wallet-friendly, and easy for autonomous transactions
2. Verifiable AI / proofs / auditability
   good fit when the chain has strong trust, verification, data integrity, or proof-oriented infrastructure
3. Decentralized compute marketplace
   good fit when the ecosystem can support supply-side compute incentives and real workload demand
4. Data provenance and model/data ownership
   good fit when the chain has strong identity, provenance, storage, or creator economy rails
5. AI consumer apps
   good fit when the chain has strong distribution, consumer wallets, cheap UX, and app ecosystem support
6. Enterprise or institutional AI workflow rails
   good fit when compliance, auditability, identity, and integration matter more than pure retail speculation
7. AI x DeFi / agent trading / autonomous on-chain execution
   good fit when the chain has deep liquidity, composability, and reliable automation rails

## Fit Questions

For each candidate direction, ask:

1. Why is this chain structurally good at this?
2. What does this chain have that competitors do not?
3. What part is blockchain actually solving instead of adding friction?
4. Is there real paying demand or just narrative demand?
5. Can this become ecosystem growth?
6. Can that ecosystem growth reach the native token?

## Output Pattern

When the request is specifically about AI fit, include:

1. `最佳结合方向`
   rank the top 1-3 AI directions for this chain
2. `为什么最适合`
   explain the structural reasons
3. `不适合的 AI 路径`
   explain which AI stories are weak fits and why
4. `落地路径`
   explain what apps, infra, partners, or developer motions would make the thesis real
5. `价值捕获`
   explain whether the token, validators, sequencer, treasury, or app layer captures value

## Common Mistakes

- assuming every chain should become an AI chain
- confusing AI branding with product-market fit
- assuming inference demand automatically belongs on-chain
- ignoring that many AI workloads are better off-chain
- ignoring distribution, wallets, liquidity, and developers when proposing AI use cases
