# Depth Framework

Use this file to make the report genuinely deep instead of merely broad.

## What "Deep" Means

A deep report does not stop at describing events. It explains:

1. what happened
2. why it matters
3. who benefits
4. who pays or gets diluted
5. what the market may be misunderstanding
6. what would prove the thesis wrong

## Mandatory Questions

Try to answer these questions somewhere in the report:

1. 核心投资逻辑是什么
   What is the shortest serious bull case?
2. 反方逻辑是什么
   What is the strongest skeptical case, not a strawman?
3. 当前价格可能已经计入了什么
   Which positives are already widely known and possibly priced in?
4. 市场可能忽略了什么
   Which variables are under-discussed, delayed, non-consensus, or structurally hard to price?
5. 一阶影响是什么
   Direct effects on users, revenue, fees, liquidity, or token demand
6. 二阶影响是什么
   Reflexivity, dilution, competitive reaction, treasury effects, regulatory second-order consequences
7. 谁在获得价值，谁在承担成本
   Users, stakers, validators, insiders, treasury, market makers, LPs, speculators, competitors
8. 未来 6-12 个月最关键的验证点是什么
   What has to happen for the thesis to strengthen?
9. 最关键的证伪点是什么
   What specific event or data would materially weaken the thesis?

## Common Failure Modes

- Listing news without analyzing its economic significance
- Using vague words like "strong ecosystem" without evidence
- Repeating the team's narrative without considering the bear case
- Confusing user growth with token-holder value capture
- Treating price momentum as fundamental validation
- Ignoring dilution, unlocks, treasury sales, or ecosystem subsidies

## Recommended Writing Pattern

For major sections, prefer this mini-structure:

1. 事实
2. 解读
3. 影响路径
4. 风险或反例

## High-Signal Additions

- `核心投资逻辑`
- `反方观点与证伪条件`
- `市场可能忽略的变量`

These sections are especially useful when the user wants the report to be deep and decision-useful.
